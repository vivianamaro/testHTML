Princess Jasmine

About Princess Jasmine:

Jasmine is incredibly independent and strong. She isn't afraid to speak her mind, no matter who she's up against; and won't hesitate to stand up for what's right, mostly for the safety of others as opposed to herself. She is extremely compassionate and caring towards her kingdom, her family, and her friends.
